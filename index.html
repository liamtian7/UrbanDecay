One building. One question.

Every round you'll see a single ruined or derelict building — no people, no captions to give it away. Decide whether you're looking at an abandoned American city (Detroit, St. Louis, Cleveland, Gary…) or a war zone (Grozny, Mosul, Aleppo, Donbas…). Then the location is unsealed.

The point of the test is how often you can't tell.

Photographs are pulled live from Wikimedia Commons and are real places. The war-damage images document real conflicts and real loss. Images are filtered to exclude people, weapons, and text where possible — if something unsuitable appears, use Skip and it won't be counted.
